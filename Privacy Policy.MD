# Privacy Policy

**Expense Tracker SMS Service**
**Last Updated: March 10, 2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Expense Tracker SMS Service ("Service," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information from users ("you" or "users") who participate in our SMS-based expense tracking system.

## Information We Collect

When you use the Service, we may collect the following information:

- **Phone number** — used to send and receive SMS messages related to expense tracking.
- **Expense data** — transaction amounts, merchant names, dates, and categories that you or other users log through the Service.
- **Receipt images** — photos of receipts sent via SMS in response to prompts from the Service.
- **Message content** — the content of SMS messages exchanged between you and the Service, including opt-in and opt-out requests.

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

- Sending expense tracking notifications, reminders, and summaries via SMS.
- Storing and organizing expense records and receipt images for your personal use.
- Processing opt-in and opt-out requests.
- Improving the functionality of the Service.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Your information may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

- **Service providers** — We use Twilio to send and receive SMS messages. Twilio processes your phone number and message content in accordance with their own privacy policy.
- **Legal requirements** — We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request.

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and access is limited to authorized administrators of the Service. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as you are an active participant in the Service. If you opt out, your phone number will be removed from the active messaging list. You may request deletion of all your data, including expense records and receipt images, at any time by contacting us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Opt out** of SMS messages at any time by replying **STOP** to any message from the Service.
- **Request access** to the personal data we hold about you.
- **Request deletion** of your personal data by contacting the Service administrator.

## Messaging Details

- **Message frequency varies** based on your expense activity and notification preferences.
- **Message and data rates may apply** depending on your mobile carrier and plan.
- Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

## Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact the Service administrator directly.
